Web & Mobile Application Development

Welcome to the Web and Mobile Application Development course at LSPU San Pablo City's Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program. This course covers web fundamentals, backend development, mobile app development, database management, UX/UI design, and application deployment. Students gain hands-on experience to design, develop, deploy, and maintain modern web and mobile applications, preparing them for careers as full-stack developers, mobile app developers, or UX/UI designers in the IT industry.

Networking

Welcome to the Networking course in LSPU San Pablo City's Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program. This course covers network fundamentals, design, security, wireless/mobile networking, administration, and emerging technologies. Students learn to design, implement, and manage modern computer networks, preparing them for careers as network administrators, engineers, cybersecurity specialists, or IT consultants in various industries.

Service MAnagement Program

Welcome to the Service Management course within LSPU San Pablo City's Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program. This course covers IT service management frameworks like ITIL, service design, operations, transition, continual service improvement, and relevant tools. Students learn how to efficiently manage IT services, align them with business objectives, and drive continual improvement. Graduates are equipped for roles such as IT service managers, service desk analysts, or IT consultants, adept at enhancing IT service delivery in various industries.

System Intellegence

Welcome to the System Intelligence course at LSPU San Pablo City's Computer Science program. This course delves into artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data mining. Students will learn the fundamentals, machine learning techniques, data mining processes, intelligent system design, and their real-world applications. Through hands-on projects, they'll gain practical skills to tackle complex problems. This course prepares students for careers in software development, data analysis, and research in the rapidly evolving field of AI and data science.
